The Fried Rice Returns

The kal raat ke chawal are back at it - transform leftover rice into mouth-melting fried rice! On medium flame, add 2 tsp ghee and prepare a tadka of zeera, sookhi laal mirch, and tez patta. Add in the (cooked) dal of your choice, stir for 30 seconds, and then add the rice. Keep stirring until a satisfactory consistency is reached. Serve topped with butter and enjoyyy!
